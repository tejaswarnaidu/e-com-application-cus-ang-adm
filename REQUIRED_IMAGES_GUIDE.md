## 🔧 **How to Add Images**

### Step 1: Get Images
- **Take photos** of actual products
- **Download** from product websites  
- **Create/design** simple product icons
- **Use stock photos** with appropriate licenses

### Step 2: Prepare Images
- **Format:** PNG (preferred) or JPG
- **Size:** 200x200px to 400x400px recommended
- **Background:** Transparent PNG or clean white background
- **Quality:** Clear, well-lit product photos

### Step 3: Add to App
1. Save images with **exact filenames** as listed above
2. Place all images in `assets/images/` directory
3. **Hot reload** your app to see images appear
